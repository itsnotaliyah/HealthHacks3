Never miss another medication

Meditrina tracks your treatment schedule for you, eliminating the need to keep tabs on your medications. In addition to receiving alerts about when to take the next pill, Meditrina will distribute the correct dosage of the correct medication into the palm of your hand. At any time you can review your medication history and refill status, and the machine will automatically send out requests for a refill when you're running low on any type of pill.

Your schedule, no sweat

As a patient, easily view which medications are perscribed by your healthcare provider, and then set a designated medication time catered to your daily agenda. That way, your schedule is easily customizable and always flexible to your needs. No need to count the numbers in your head, just wait for the notification and be provided with treatments at your own pace.

Adaptable for every patient's needs

No matter the drug or disability, Meditrina's simple yet innovative design is guaranteed to be the easiest pill distributer across all types of users. Obtaining the medication requires nothing more than a light tap on the underside of the dispenser, delivering the correct dosage to your palm or personal medicinal aid. Need more assistance? We provide our files open source for easy modifications to the system itself, as well as customizable aids that can be built with 3D printing technology.

Easily view your stats

At any time you can view a detailed report on your treatment history, as well as any missed medications, inventory, and current pill schedule.

Share analytics to your provider

Connect your in depth report from Meditrina with your caretaker or physician at all times, transforming the way healthcare providers are able to look after and care for their patients.

Amazon Alexa

Connect your Amazon Echo device to receive further notifications about when to take your medications with Alexa, including alerts for when a certain medication is running low or needs to be refilled.

Mobile Devices

Provide your phone number to obtain text message notifications when it is time to take your next pill, as well as refill status and pill inventory.
